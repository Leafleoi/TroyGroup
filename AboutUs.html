About Us

Welcome to the Trojan Cafe! We strive to serve the best food and beverages for all students at affordable prices!
Our cafe is fully staffed by Troy students who are trained in crafting the perfect drink to get you through your classes.
We accept cash, all major credit cards as well as any University Dining Plan. We are also open late on weekdays and have
extended hours during finals week to give you that extra boost for studying.
